Journey Through Japan - An event sponsered by the Atomic Testing Museum held 6 August 2011

The Atomic Testing Museum in collaboration with the Japan American Society of Nevada, Metro Arts Council and Consulate General of Japan in Las Vegas put together a day of Taiko, Kendo, Tea, Origami, Ikebana and and a Lantern Ceremony. The Temple participated in the event and spearheaded the creation of Toro Lanterns and floating ceremony at Sunset Park. Click the above link for more information and pictures.
