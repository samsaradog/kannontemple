OFFERINGS

 

Incense

Incense are offered during the service for purification of the three poisons (Greed, Anger, Ignorance).

Candle

Candle light is offered to represent the Buddha's enlightenment. The light brightens the dark.

 

Fresh Flowers

Flowers are offered so we may remember impermanence. They are beautiful and pleasing to the eye but we always remember that they will fade, grow old and die.

 

Other Offerings

Other items such as food, water and tea may be offered on the altar. When we receive a guest we always offer refreshment.

Golden Lotus Blossoms

Many Temple Altars have golden lotus blossoms. As you look upon the beauty of the golden lotus flowers you see the cycle of samsara (cycle of birth and death). Amongst the blossoms is a bud, the beginning of life. Then you will notice a full flower in all its beauty. Then one of the flowers has started to loose its petals in old age. Finally we see the seed pod. The end of life but containing the seed (potential) of new life.
